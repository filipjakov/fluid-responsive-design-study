vmin

Relative to viewport’s smaller dimension (e.g. for portrait orientation, the width is smaller than the height so it’s relative to the width). 1vmin = 1/100 of viewport’s smaller dimension.

Think of it as this:

100vmin = 100vw or 100vh, whichever is smaller
1vmin = 1vw or 1vh, whichever is smaller

Sweet little use-case:

Fluid header:

header { padding: 10vmin 1rem; }

vmax

Relative to viewport’s larger dimension (e.g. height for portrait orientation). 1vmax = 1/100 of viewport’s larger dimension.

Think of it as this:

100vmax = 100vw or 100vh, whichever is larger
1vmax = 1vw or 1vh, whichever is larger
