ex

Represents the x-height of the element's font. On fonts with the "x" letter, this is generally the height of lowercase letters in the font; 1ex ≈ 0.5em in many fonts.

Unlike ems, which don’t change when you change the font-family, ex units do change when you change the font-family, as the value of one unit is specifically bound to that font:
