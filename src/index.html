Motivation

Design types

  • fixed page layout - designed for a specific predetermined width and they do not change, no matter the display aspect ratio or the screen size that the viewer has
  • adaptive/responsive design - process of creating a website or a theme with specific devices and screen sizes in mind.
  • fluid design - fluid or liquid design has the width specified as a percentage. It is based on relative units and proportional widths, making pages scalable and adjustable to various screen sizes.
